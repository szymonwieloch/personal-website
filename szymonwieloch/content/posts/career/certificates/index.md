## Formal Education
![](./AGH.png)
**AGH - Academy Of Science And Technology ([agh.edu.pl](http://agh.edu.pl))**  
**Electronics And Telecommunications**  
**minor: Telecommunications**

Bachelor’s and Master’s studies in electronics and networks with a specialization in telecommunications.

AGH University of Krakow is one of Poland's top technical powerhouses, famous for its prestigious engineering programs and a vibrant, central campus. At its cutting edge, the Faculty of Computer Science, Electronics and Telecommunications holds a top-tier scientific rating and works closely with global tech leaders. Their Electronics and Telecommunications program perfectly bridges high-performance hardware design with complex network architecture, making its graduates exceptionally sought-after in modern technology sectors.

![](./logo_wsb-nlu_1.png)

**Wyższa Szkoła Biznesu — National Louis University ([wsb-nlu.edu.pl](https://wsb-nlu.edu.pl))**  
**Master of Business Administration — postgraduate study program**

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) program at Wyższa Szkoła Biznesu - National Louis University (WSB-NLU) is highly regarded for its innovative, practice-oriented approach to developing top-tier global leaders. Taught by industry experts and tailored for working professionals, the program seamlessly blends theoretical strategic frameworks with real-world managerial problem-solving.

A standout feature of this prestigious program is its unique double-diploma tracking; upon successful completion, graduates are awarded two distinct credentials: an **American MBA diploma issued by National Louis University in Chicago** and a **prestigious certificate of completion of postgraduate studies from WSB-NLU in Poland**. This dual qualification provides alumni with an exceptional competitive edge and a highly recognized global credential on the international job market.

![](./logo-WSE.png)

**Wyższa Szkoła Europejska, Cracow ([wse.krakow.pl](wse.krakow.pl))**  
**Postgraduate study program in coaching and training**

The "Coach i Trener" postgraduate program at Tischner European University (WSE) in Kraków offers a powerful dual-competency approach designed to master both individual development and group dynamics. Focusing heavily on an experiential, workshop-driven curriculum, the program transforms academic theory into highly actionable skills for personal and organizational growth.

A central highlight of the program is its rigorous alignment with top-tier international professional frameworks. Graduates emerge thoroughly prepared to structure training and development initiatives according to European **EI (Edexcel International) vocational standards**, while simultaneously mastering coaching protocols that align with the global ethical guidelines of the **International Coach Federation (ICF)**.
